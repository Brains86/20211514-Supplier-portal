Handy help with the Supplier Portal of ANWB Reizen

As we have previously informed you, with the introduction of our new ANWB website, we will switch to one portal, the Supplier Portal, for all communication regarding reservations, requests and reports. Our intention is to limit "loose" email traffic by means of this Supplier Portal and to keep all communication organized in an orderly way.

For this new Supplier Portal you have received an email from our Operations department with a link to activate your account in the Supplier Portal. If you have not received this email, please check your spam box. If you haven’t received the email at all, please contact supplierportal@anwbreizen.nl.
Each supplier has 1 account that is linked to 1 email address.

Every partner/ supplier whose trips we have already published on our new ANWB website will receive all information regarding new bookings via this Supplier Portal. You will therefore no longer receive the bookings through our old systems (Lanterna, Extranet or Mutation lists). Already existing bookings are still available in the old systems. You will therefore temporarily have to keep an eye on two systems.

We wish you good luck using this new Supplier Portal!
Questions about the portal? Mail directly to supplierportal@anwbreizen.nl
Questions about reservations? Then place a memo within the Supplier Portal or email your regular Operations contact.
